# OpenAI Image Prompting Best Practices

These guidelines help produce higher-quality images with OpenAI image generation models.

## Best Practices

1. **Be specific**: Include clear subject details and attributes.
2. **Use descriptive language**: Add color, texture, lighting, and mood.
3. **Set context**: Describe the scene, background, or environment.
4. **Specify style or medium**: Example: photorealistic, watercolor, cinematic, 3D render.
5. **Include action or emotion**: Adds energy and intent.
6. **Highlight key elements**: Call out must-have objects or features.
7. **Control complexity**: Simple prompts for clarity, detailed prompts for richness.
8. **Iterate and refine**: Adjust prompts based on outputs.
9. **Include composition details**: Example: close-up, wide shot, rule of thirds.
10. **Mention output format**: Example: square, landscape, portrait.
